MVN | Shop Settings

This is the Shop Settings Station.

This is the place where you decide what happens to all the products.

Some of the things you have control of are as follows:

- Manage Products -> CRUD Operations

->"View" button - Redirects to product page.

->"Edit" button - Edit product details.

->"Delete" button - Deletes Product.


Here you can also Create a new Product.

MVN | Blog Settings

This is the Blog Settings Station.

This is the place where you decide what happens to all the posts(articles).

Some of the things you have control of are as follows:

- Manage Posts -> CRUD Operations

->"View" button - Redirects to post.

->"Edit" button - Edit post credentials.

->"Delete" button - Deletes post and everything connected with it.


Here you can also Create a new Post.
